Tool which allows dragging creatures in campaign using your mouse. Also includes options to pause and delete individual creatures and objects. Requires Dev Tools. 

Source code is here: https://github.com/woutkolkman/mousedrag 

[h3]Known bugs[/h3]
- Pausing a deer while you're on it will make you slide off and be unable to move.
